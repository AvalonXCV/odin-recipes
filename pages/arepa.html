Arepa Recipe

The arepa is a traditional dish of Colombia. Made from ground dry corn dough or precooked corn flour, circular and flattened in shape. It is known under this name in Colombia and Venezuela.

Ingredients

  • Corn flavor
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Preparation

  1. Heat 250ml of water to a warm temperature.
  2. Put the half cup of flour in a bowl.
  3. Drop the water in the bowl and let the flour hidratate
  4. Add a pinch of salt
  5. Add 20 g of butter to the mix.
  6. Mix with the hands to homogenize.
  7. After the mix is complete, make some balls with the dough.
  8. Flat the balls and put them on a pan.
  9. Let them cook, and serve with cheese
